ParKING

The Project

WHO
Drivers who are finding car parks or meters

WHAT
A location-based responsive web app that shows the details of parking information

PROBLEM
Drivers are frustrated when finding parking slots because they are not well-informed the availability and the price of the parking slots: meters and car parks

SOLUTION
To build an app, ParKING, that well-informing users about the realtime availability and the price of parking slots nearby, as well as acting as a payment platform, allowing users to pay on the app.
