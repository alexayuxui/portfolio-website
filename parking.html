ParKING

The Project

In my UX/UI course, I was asked to design a location-based responsive web app. The reason I chose to build a parking app is there is no such app in my home country, Hong Kong, and this frustrated me many times in the past.


WHO
Drivers who are finding car parks or meters

WHAT
A location-based responsive web app that shows the details of parking information

PROBLEM
Drivers are frustrated when finding parking slots because they are not well-informed the availability and the price of the parking slots: meters and car parks.

SOLUTION
To build an app, ParKING, that well-informing users about the real time availability and the price of parking slots nearby, as well as acting as a payment platform, allowing users to pay on the app.
