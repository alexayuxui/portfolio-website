Objective
ParKING is a location-based responsive web app that helps drivers find their parking slots. The app shows the location of parking slots in a map with real time availability and price. It also act as a payment platform that allows users to pay on the app.


Context
The idea for ParKING came about when I was finding parking slots in Hong Kong as a site-visiting Sales representative. In the city, the parking slots in shopping mall giants are space-guaranteed but very expensive, whereas those cheaper ones are full most of the time. There are even no apps showing the locations of meters.

Both my colleagues and I suffered a lot from pricy parking or time-consuming parking slot searching.

This made me think that it would be helpful to have an app that could provide information like price and real time availability for car parks and meters.

User Interview

I conducted an interview with 6 targeted users. The purpose was to figure out the painpoints and concerns on finding parking slots in the city.

Competitive Analysis

After learning the user’s patterns, I analysed the apps they have mentioned.

This helps me understand the emphaty of user thinking and learn where I should focus on.

ParKING could inform users the availability of parking slots before they go for illegal parking.
